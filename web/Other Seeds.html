Grasses make a great addition to any garden design. Whether you are looking to plant a container garden or transform a hillside, grasses will fit the bill. Grasses are very well suited for containers. They can be used as a tall centerpiece in an urn or planted solo in an over sized container. Flowering grasses are considered very beneficial for the landscaper.We provide seeds of top easy to grow and maintain grasses for you. They offer both beauty and function, giving your yard color while simultaneously helping you fight weeds and control erosion. We have Bermuda Grass, Stylosanthes hamata Grass, Cenchrus ciliaris, Buffel Grass, Deenanath Grass seeds and more. If you are seeking a solution for a problem area, ground covers can get the job done without making you compromise on display value. A good ground cover is one that requires the least maintenance, yet does not look uncultured or weedy. It acts as an insulating cover for the soil, keeping it cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.
