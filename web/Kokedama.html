Kokedama

Kokedama is a ball of soil, covered with moss, in which an ornamental plant grows. It is Japanese art but, now-a-days it is being more popular everywhere because of its truly unique way of displaying your favorite plants and creating an indoor plant feature. This craft technique is the sister of Bonsai. Kokedama has certain similarities with bonsai, seeking to recreate a natural habitat that fosters relaxation and well-being environments. Kokedama, like any decorative plant, help to purify the air and create a relaxed atmosphere, which means a reduction in the feeling of stress. You can create Kokedama by using different plant varieties like shrubs, Indoor plants, flowering plants, in original and ecological form and much more natural than anything else. Kokedama can enhance the beauty of your house, office, balcony, just hang them and enjoy the greenery around you. .
